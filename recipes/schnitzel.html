Schnitzel with french fries

Description

Wiener Schnitzel with french fries and ketchup.

Ingredients

  • 200 g fry butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 150g flour
  • 300g breadcrumbs
  • 4 veal escalope
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Beat the veal reasonably thin, put salt and pepper on both sides of it
  2. Prepare 3 plates with each the flour, whisked eggs and breadcrumbs
  3. Firstly put the veal in the flour (both sides), then run it through the eggs and afterwards put it into the breadcrumbs.
  4. Put as much fry butter in pan so that the veals swim in it and fry them until they are golden.
  5. Cut lemon in pieces and place on veal
  6. Bake french fries
