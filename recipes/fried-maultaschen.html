Fried Maultaschen

Description

Swabian fried Maultaschen. Basically pasta filled with meat and/or spinach. Fried in a pan with eggs.

Ingredients

  • 400g Maultaschen
  • 2 eggs
  • Sunflower/Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Optional a sauce e.g. tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Put Maultaschen in pan with oil
  2. Fry them until they are cripsy to your liking
  3. Add the eggs
  4. Add salt and pepper
  5. Optionally make sauce
