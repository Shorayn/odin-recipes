Sardines salad

Description

Sardine salad with tomato and cucumber.

Ingredients

  • 2 Oil-sardines cans
  • 1 Tomato
  • Half a cucumber
  • Olive oil
  • Vinegar
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Cut tomato and cucumber in small pieces and put in bowl
  2. Add the sardines to the bowl
  3. Added oil, vinegar, salt and pepper
  4. Mix everything
